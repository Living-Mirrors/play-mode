**Start here.** Then:

1. **FRAMEWORK.md** — Understand the six research programs that underpin the specification. This is the theoretical ground.
2. **SYNTHESIS.md** — See how researchers across different fields and decades arrived at compatible conclusions about play. This is the intellectual centerpiece.
3. **RECIPROCITY.md** — The unique document. A deep investigation of the 30% rule — the experimental evidence from rat rough-and-tumble play research and its implications for designing AI interaction that sustains co-creation. This is what Play Mode contributes that no other mode does.
4. **DESIGN-DECISIONS.md** — Verify that every design choice in the spec has an evidence trail. This is the accountability document.
5. **papers/** — Go deep on any individual source. Each file is self-contained with abstract, methodology, key findings, and relevance to Play Mode.

## Selection Methodology

Papers and books were selected according to four criteria:

1. **Direct relevance.** The work must address the mechanisms of play — its neurobiology, developmental function, cultural significance, or social dynamics. Not play therapy, not gamification, not "fun at work." The question is: *what happens in the brain and between two players when play is occurring, and what are the conditions under which it flourishes or dies?*

2. **Empirical grounding or theoretical primacy.** Each source is either (a) an empirical study with reproducible methodology, (b) a theoretical work that introduced a concept subsequently validated by empirical research, or (c) a foundational text that defined a research program. Popular science summaries and secondary literature were excluded except where the original researcher wrote the popular account (Brown, 2009; Peterson, 2018).

3. **Independence.** The six research programs represented here were conducted independently — different decades, different fields, different methodologies. This independence is what makes their convergence significant. Sources were selected to represent genuinely distinct lines of inquiry, not a single tradition citing itself.

4. **Specificity of contribution.** Each source must provide something no other source in the collection provides. Redundant coverage was eliminated. Where multiple works address the same phenomenon, the one with the strongest methodology or the most direct relevance to the specification was retained.

## Citation Format

All citations follow APA 7th edition format. DOI links are provided where available. For books, publisher and ISBN are specified. For journal articles, volume, issue, and page numbers are included.

In-text references throughout the research directory use author-date format: (Panksepp, 1998), (Huizinga, 1938), (Pellis & Pellis, 2007).
